Four Dimensions of Analysis

🧠

Aesthetic Score

Deep CNN features mapped to a 1–10 aesthetic score using patterns from 250k+ rated photographs from the AVA dataset.

⚖️

Composition

Rule-of-thirds grid analysis with saliency-based subject detection. Measures proximity to power points.

☀️

Lighting

Histogram, mean brightness, highlight & shadow clipping analysis. Classifies exposure and gives targeted feedback.

🎯

Sharpness

Laplacian variance + gradient orientation to detect defocus vs motion blur, then maps to a perceptual sharpness score.
